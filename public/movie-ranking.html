My Favorite Books of the year

My top 3 books from this year's readings

Final offer - Lauren Asher

This is my top favorite book this year.
I gave it 5 stars without even finish it.
I related so much to both of the main characters, I cried A LOT each time i read something about the characters' traumas.
It was so heartbreaking yet beautiful and entertaining.
